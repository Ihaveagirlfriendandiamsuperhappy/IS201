There's A New Dream Team On The Block.

We're Talented, We're Scrappy, We're Hungry, and We're Coming For a T-Shirt

Rome wasn't built in a day... Neither was the Dream Team

The pick-up bALL STARS have lived on the cusp of greatness. In both of our first two seasons we were knocked out in the Final Four. Most intramural teams would be incredibly satisfied with two consecuative top four finishes... but we're like hungry sharks who can smell blood in the water! So here's what the off-season looked like:

  • We reworked the roster. We made some great trades and built around our core.
  • We lived in the gym. There is no subsitute for hard work and reps.
  • We tore apart our film. We found the weaknesses and turned them into strengths.

There's really only one thing that we need and, YOU are the only one that can help us. The only thing we are lacking is a faithful, devoted fan base that we be with us from game one -> alllll the way until we kiss that trophy and do snow angels in that confetti. So what do you say?? Be our 6th man and lets go get that T-SHIRT

Core Principles and Focuses

Team First

You do your job, and we'll win games. Everyone has a different role, and when we unite, we're unstoppable.

Be A Playmaker

Confidence is key. If you have an open shot, then shoot it! Be an athelete and let your competitive spark light our whole team on fire.

Defense, Defense, Defense.

Shooting will come and go. The refs might not have your back every game. Your fan base might all get food poisoning on the same day and miss your game. the only thing that will never fail us must be our defense.

Score More Points Than The Other Team

The Sam Stevenson Special. A principle that we live by.
